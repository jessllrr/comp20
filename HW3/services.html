About Us

Flower Shower merges both virtual and physical mediums in flower arrangement and delivery. With our cutting-edge rendering technology, our experienced artisans and florists and our dedicated workers, we create a unique new experience that brings the experience of receiving flowers to a larger audience. Our greenhouses are famous in their regions for their environmental efforts in increasing tree-ownership and sustainable fashion accessories.

Our Services

The Digital Bouquet

At Flower Shower, we have an entire digital selection of flowers to choose from. For those less inclined with flower-care, the Digital Bouquet is the perfect solution. This option comes with not only a digitally arranged bouquet but a handcrafted painting of your selected arrangement.

$ 30

Arrangement Delivery

Our most popular service is the delivery of a bouquet hand picked by our clients and delivered to the client's recipient. These flowers can range from a simple corsage to an elegant bouquet to a complex arrangement. Each delivery comes with a handcrafted note and a blown glass vase.

$ 50

The Flower Shoppe

Along with our digital and delivery options, Flower Shower has two greenhouses locations in Chicago, IL and Boston, MA. At our greenhouses, we offer surveying and delivery services for trees, as well as flower pressing and jewelry making options. Come visit us!

Prices at each location may vary.
